Study Summary & Context

Data from Open Data - Comune di Roma: Incidenti stradali nel territorio di Roma Capitale 2013-2022, filtered to pedestrian accidents involving one moving vehicle.

Accidents took place during the period 01/01/2013 – 31/08/2022. Accidents with missing information have been removed.

Priority Hotspots Map: Spatial clustering (DBSCAN) highlights “blackspots” by combining accident frequencies and pedestrian injury severities.

Injury Levels

  • 0 - Uninjured (but involved in the accident)
  • 1 - Slight injury
  • 2 - Moderate injury (hospitalised)
  • 3 - Moderate/severe injury, died later
  • 4 - Died instantly
